Specialising in motorsport electrical systems with 25 years experience at the top level of the sport

RT Motorsport Wiring aims to produce wiring and electrical/electronic systems using the most up to date techniques and materials. We believe that we can produce the best electrical systems to fit your requirements and budget.

We primarily use Raychem wire, heatshrink and moulded parts because these are proven as the best products for the market. However, we can use alternative products to suit your budget.

We recommend the use of Deutsch and Souriau connectors due to their lightweight and reliable properties. These connectors are specifically designed for the motorsport, defence, marine and aerospace industries.

We can build batches of harnesses or supply on-site labour at an agreed daily or hourly rate.
